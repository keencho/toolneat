Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Should I choose MP4 or WEBM?

MP4 is compatible with all devices. WEBM saves faster but may not play on some devices.

Q: Can I record microphone audio?

Yes, check 'Include Microphone' to record your voice. Great for tutorials.

Q: Why doesn't MP4 conversion work?

MP4 conversion requires HTTPS. On HTTP, only WEBM format is available.

Q: Is there a recording time limit?

No technical limit, but long recordings use more browser memory. Consider splitting recordings over 30 minutes.

All processing is done in your browser. No data uploaded to servers.
