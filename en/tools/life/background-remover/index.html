AI Background Removal

AI recognizes subjects in your image and automatically removes the background. Great for e-commerce, profile photos, and social media content.

How to Use

  1. Drag or click to upload an image
  2. AI automatically analyzes and removes the background
  3. Adjust options: background color, format, size
  4. Click 'Download' to save the result

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What images work best?

Images with clear distinction between subject and background work best. Portrait and product photos are ideal. Hair and complex edges are handled well.

Q: Why does it take time to process?

The AI model (~88MB) downloads on first use. After that, it's cached for faster processing.

Q: How do I save with transparent background?

Select 'Transparent' for background color and choose PNG format. JPG doesn't support transparency.

Q: Is my image uploaded to servers?

No, all processing is 100% in your browser. Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy.
